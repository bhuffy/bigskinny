The Super Skinny is 1/2 as thin as our other designs and 4 times thinner than a typical leather wallet with the same amount of cards and cash. All the extra-wide pockets can fit a total of 30-36 full-size plastic cards and you still have the billfold area for cash, receipts, etc.

Pockets and billfold area are lined with a rubbery coating to help prevent cards and cash from sliding out and a horizontal-style card layout to prevent cards from sliding out.

The Super Skinny is designed with “joints” so the sections move & bend along with your body for more comfort despite its larger size.
